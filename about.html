As a designer with 10+ years in art direction, I meet business needs while creating compelling user experiences.

Who I am

I’m creative. I love gathering inspiration and generating ideas. I thrive in conveying complex ideas through clean, simple imagery.

I’m investigative. I ask a lot of questions. I spot patterns, find themes and make connections.

I’m analytical. I enjoy examining and evaluating data that leads to sound decisions and innovative problem solving.

Nothing brings me more joy than creating solutions that change people’s lives. Immersing myself in finding those solutions comes in a close second.

In my everyday life, I strive to lead a healthy lifestyle. I practice yoga, meditation, latin ballroom dancing and cycling. Doting on my cats and discussing life with my husband daily keeps me grounded and balanced.
